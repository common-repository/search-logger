Keep an eye on what your site visitors are looking for.

== Description ==

It's always super cool to read your visitors' mind. With this plugin, you can keep an eye on what your site visitors are looking for.

This plugin beautifully keeps log of search queries made on your site. What visitors are searching for, what results they got and even who searches that.

== Installation ==

1. Install `Search Logger` using the native Plugin installer, or download the zip and extract it in the wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` admin menu.
3. Configure from the settings page

Please [reach out to us](https://help.codexpert.io/?utm_campaign=wporg) if you need any help.
